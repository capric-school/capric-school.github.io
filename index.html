About CAPRIC

CAPRIC is an international doctoral school dedicated to advancing research in Cyber-Physical Cloud Systems. Our mission is to train highly skilled researchers who can tackle complex interdisciplinary challenges combining cyber systems, physical devices, and cloud computing.

Research Areas

  • Cloud-Integrated Sensor Networks
  • Real-Time Cyber-Physical Systems
  • AI and Machine Learning for Embedded Systems
  • Security and Privacy in Cloud CPS
  • Edge Computing and Distributed Architectures

Program Structure

The CAPRIC program includes foundational courses, advanced seminars, interdisciplinary workshops, and international collaborations. Students will engage in innovative research guided by leading experts in the field.
